TITLE: Community News submissions page

Welcome to the home of the CONNECT Community News.
CONNECT is a trusted source of information for international research and education networking news. To maintain this quality, please follow these essential guidelines.
News Submissions
Image Requirements:
Images must be
at least 1000 pixels wide
and relevant to the article.
Project or company logos must be added manually.
Relevance: Only upload news that you think will be relevant / of interest to an international audience.
Newsletter Mentions: Highlight just 1–2 global stories, not the entire edition.
Headline: Keep it
8–10 words maximum
, in English.
Abstract: Max 150 words. Do not paste the full article.
URL: Must link to a public, non-password-protected full article.
Language Note: All news headlines and teaser texts must be in English.  If the article is in another language, add: “More in [language]” at the end.
Author: The “Your Name” field should name
the original author
.
Image Use – Copyright & GDPR
Use only
images you have full rights to:
Licensed stock or Creative Commons (check permissions),
Internally created or rights-confirmed visuals.
Do not use
:
Images found online,
AI-generated content without clear rights or licences.
Attribution
: Required if the licence (e.g. CC BY) mandates it.
AI Images
: Allowed only with full rights and no infringement of third-party likeness or style.
People in Photos
:
If clearly identifiable, obtain informed consent unless an exception applies (e.g. public event).
Avoid or anonymize (e.g. blur faces) when in doubt.
*Please note: if you are logged into the website then the “Your Name” field is pre-populated and hidden. If you wish to post an article on behalf of another author then please log out of the site before submitting a post to CONNECT.

Who is the Data Controller?
The data controller is:
GÉANT Association
(Hoekenrode 3, 1102 BR Amsterdam, The Netherlands). Controller has appointed DPO who can be contacted via email:
gdpr@geant.org
Purpose of Data Collection
When you submit an article and/or image for publication in CONNECT Magazine/Blog, we collect and process personal data for the following purposes:
To review, edit, and publish your article
To credit you as the author
To contact you regarding your submission
To promote CONNECT Magazine/Blog or website through GÉANT’s digital channels (e.g. website, social media, newsletters)
What Personal Data Is Collected?
We may collect and process:
Your full name and professional affiliation
Email address and contact details
Biography or author description (if provided)
Submitted images and their metadata
Any other personal data you include in your article
How Is Your Data Used and Shared?
Your article, name, and related images may be published in both print and digital editions of CONNECT Magazine/Blog and/or connect.geant.org).
Content may be republished or shared via GÉANT’s website (connect.geant.org), social media, and other communication channels.
Your personal data is processed only by authorised GÉANT staff and third-party service providers (e.g. printing or design services), solely for the purposes above.
Data Retention
Your submission and related personal data may be retained for archival, editorial, or legal purposes
Your Rights Under GDPR
As an individual, you have the right to: access your personal data, request correction or deletion, object to or restrict processing, lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise your rights, contact:
gdpr@geant.org
